Gone Without Goodbye

When trust shatters, what's left behind?

For Kacey, everything begins and ends with Maddie. Since they were five years old, the two were inseparable — sharing secrets, dreams, and the quiet certainty that nothing could ever pull them apart. But when Maddie goes to the biggest party of the summer and doesn't come back, Kacey's perfect world fractures into something far darker.

As the hours stretch into days, Kacey embarks on a desperate search that leads her into a web of secrets, lies, and disturbing revelations about the people closest to her. The closer she gets to the truth, the more she realizes that someone is watching — someone who doesn't want Maddie to be found.

With each new piece of the puzzle, Kacey is forced to question everything she thought she knew about her best friend, their town, and even herself. In the end, she must confront a chilling truth: some secrets are meant to be buried.
